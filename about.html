About Shape Shifter Duel

Game Concept

Shape Shifter Duel is a retro-inspired arcade shooter that combines fast-paced action with strategic shape-shifting mechanics. As the player, you control a ship that can transform between three different shapes: Circle, Triangle, and Cube.

The core gameplay revolves around matching your shape to enemy types for maximum damage. This creates a dynamic combat system where quick reflexes and strategic decisions are equally important.

Development Story

The game was originally developed as a Java application and has since been converted to a web-based JavaScript game with HTML5 Canvas. This transition has made the game more accessible to players across different platforms.

Shape Shifter Duel draws inspiration from classic arcade games like Galaga and Space Invaders, but adds its own unique twist with the shape-shifting mechanic. The retro-style visuals and sound effects pay homage to the golden age of arcade gaming.
