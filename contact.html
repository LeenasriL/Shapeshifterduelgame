Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shape Shifter Duel free to play?

Yes, the web-based version of Shape Shifter Duel is completely free to play.

Does the game save my progress?

Progress is saved automatically if you're playing while logged in to your account.

Is there a mobile version available?

The web version is responsive and can be played on mobile browsers.

How do I report a bug?

You can report bugs through the contact form by selecting "Bug Report" as the subject.
