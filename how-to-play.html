How to Play Shape Shifter Duel

Game Modes

Single Player Mode

Progress through 5 levels of increasing difficulty with specific objectives.

  • Each level has a point goal to advance
  • Enemy patterns become more complex with each level
  • Weapon upgrades unlock as you progress

To Start: S at the main menu, then select a level (1-5)

Duel Mode

Compete against another player locally on the same computer.

  • Two players battle each other in a strategic duel
  • Players can pick up power-ups to gain advantages
  • The first player to defeat the other wins

To Start: D at the main menu, then select a level (1-5)

Shape-Shifting Strategy

The core gameplay mechanic involves matching your shape to enemy types for maximum damage:

Circle Shape

Effective against Circle enemies

Balanced speed and handling

Triangle Shape

Effective against Triangle enemies

Faster movement but less durability

Cube Shape

Effective against Cube enemies

Slower but more powerful attacks

When your shape matches an enemy's shape, you deal critical damage (instant defeat). Watch for the yellow highlight around matching enemies!

Power-ups

Health

Restores 25 health points

Shield

Provides 5 seconds of invulnerability

Speed

Increases movement speed for 10 seconds
